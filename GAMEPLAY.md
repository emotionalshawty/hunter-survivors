# Hunter Survivors - Playable Demo

## How to Play

### Objective
Survive as long as possible while defeating waves of enemies! Gain experience to level up and increase your power.

### Controls
- **Movement**: Arrow Keys or WASD
- **Attack**: Automatic (targets nearest enemy)

### Gameplay Mechanics

**Player**
- Move around to avoid enemies
- Automatically shoots projectiles at the nearest enemy
- Take damage when touched by enemies
- Level up by gaining XP from defeating enemies

**Enemies**
- Spawn periodically and chase the player
- Deal contact damage when touching the player
- Drop XP when defeated

**Progression**
- Level up by collecting XP
- Fire rate increases with each level
- Enemy spawn rate increases over time
- Enemies become faster and tougher as time goes on

### Goal
Survive as long as possible and achieve the highest score!

## Running the Game

1. Open the project in Godot 4.6 or later
2. Press F5 or click the Play button
3. Start surviving!

## Game Over
When your HP reaches 0, the game ends and displays your final score.
