How to Play

Survive as long as possible

1

Create your account

Register with email and pick a pilot name. Your stats and scores are saved to the cloud from your first run.

2

Move to survive

Enemies spawn and chase you constantly. Keep moving — standing still means taking contact damage from every direction.

3

Collect XP & level up

Defeated enemies drop XP orbs. Walk over them to collect, fill the bar, and choose your next upgrade at each level threshold.

4

Beat your high score

Your score equals coins earned. Beating your personal best updates the global leaderboard automatically.
